# Design Brainstorm — English With Barry

## Context
A professional Italian English teacher (Barry, Australian native speaker) based in Padova, offering courses for adults and kids. The site is in Italian. The brand personality: warm, dynamic, sporty, modern, trustworthy.

**Design Movement:** Warm Modernism meets Mediterranean Energy

**Core Principles:**
1. Warmth through earthy amber/terracotta tones offset by deep ocean navy — evoking both Australia and Italy

- Hero: Full-bleed diagonal split — left side image, right side bold headline text
- Sections alternate between left-heavy and right-heavy content blocks
- Cards use subtle drop shadows and slight rotation for a "pinned" feel
- Testimonials in a horizontal scrolling strip

**Signature Elements:**
1. Diagonal section dividers (clip-path) separating content zones
2. Large quotation marks as decorative elements in testimonials
3. Wavy underlines on key phrases

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Smooth scroll with section entrance animations (fade + slide up)
- Hover states on cards: subtle lift + border color change
- CTA buttons: fill-sweep animation on hover

**Animation:**
- Hero text: staggered word-by-word entrance
- Section cards: fade-in-up with 100ms stagger between items
- Navigation: sticky with background blur on scroll

**Typography System:**
- Display: Playfair Display (serif) — for headlines, emotional weight
- Body: DM Sans — clean, modern, highly readable
- Accent: DM Mono — for labels, badges, course codes

## Selected Design: **Warm Modernism meets Mediterranean Energy** (Response 1)

This approach best reflects Barry's dual identity (Australian + Italian context), his sporty/dynamic personality, and the professional yet warm tone needed for a language school. The navy + amber palette is distinctive without being aggressive, and the editorial layout will feel significantly more premium than the current Google Sites page.
